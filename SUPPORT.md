# Supporting Bebop

Bebop is free software under AGPL-3.0. If it helps you and you'd like to support the maintainer
and his true, chosen family:

- The maintainer is currently **blocked from all money-receiving platforms** due to collective
  personal targeting. Direct platform donations (GitHub Sponsors, Patreon, etc.) are not
  currently available.
- To help safely, **open an issue or use the GitHub profile contact** and the maintainer will
  share a current, safe channel. Please do not send card numbers or banking details into public
  spaces — they get scraped and abused.

Every contribution — code, docs, issue triage, or simply spreading the word — is welcome and
appreciated. Hybrid is a feature, not a bug.
